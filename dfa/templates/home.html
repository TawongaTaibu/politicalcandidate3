Our Manifesto

DFA is proud to announce that we managed to win half of the parliamentary seats during the last election and we are going to continue delivering tangible results to our constituencies. Moving forward, our key focus is addressing the global warming challenges that our country is facing as this undermines the gross food harvest we expect to fight against hunger in our beloved nation. We strongly believe that through collaborations with both the public and private sectors, this challenge will become something of the past. On another note, DFA through it's members of parliament addressed the issue of Gender Based Violence in our country and how much it can affect the overall GDP of our motherland. These issues should be dealt with thoroughly by our law enforcement officers.
